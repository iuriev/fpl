App Store compliance note

Apple/Google typically require clarity that the app is unofficial and not affiliated with the Premier League. Use footer copy: “Unofficial Fan App — not affiliated with the Premier League or Fantasy Premier League.” Keep “FPL” in metadata keywords sparingly; avoid it in the primary logo wordmark if PL counsel flags it.
